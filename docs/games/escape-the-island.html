Escape the Island

Five puzzles. Three chances. No rescue team.

Your charter plane went down somewhere over the Pacific. You woke up alone on a beach with nothing but a waterlogged notebook and a stubborn will to survive. The island is small but full of secrets left by castaways who came before you.

Five locations hold the clues you need to reach the old shipwreck on the north shore — and the boat that just might get you home. Solve every puzzle. Manage your energy. One wrong move too many and the island wins.

You have 3 energy bars. Each wrong answer costs one. Reach zero and it's game over. Good luck — you'll need it.
